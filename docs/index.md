Ares-LoRa is LoRa communication Platform. It consists of the firmware and the serial driver to communicate with the 
firmware.

## LoRa Network

The LoRa network consists of `n` amount of nodes, with one of the nodes being a master node. The messages sent
are sent a certain amount of times with the hope that they will be received by all the nodes. See 
[Communications](communications/index.md#lora-protocol) for more details.
